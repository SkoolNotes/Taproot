> Suppose $T \in  \mathcal{L}(V)$ and $U$ is a subspace of $V$. Prove
> that $U$ is invariant under $T$ iff $U^\bot$ is invariant under $T^*$.

For all $u \in  U$, $Tu = u' \in  U$.

This implies that both directions, since $U = U^{\perp ^\perp }$ and
$T = (T^*)^*$.
