## Problem 4
### $(a)$
$$
\begin{aligned}
V &= 24.0Lmol^{-1} \\
V(t) &= 24t \\
R(t) &= \sqrt[3]{\frac{3}{4}V(t)} \\
&= \sqrt[3]{18t} \\
t &= 3 \\
V(3) &= 72L \\
R(3) &= 3\sqrt[3]{2} * 10cm &= 30\sqrt[3]{2} cm\\
V'(t) &= 24 \\
R'(t) &= \frac{18}{\sqrt[3]{18t}^2} \\
V'(3) &= 24 Ls^{-1} \\
R'(3) &= \frac{18}{\sqrt[3]{18(3)}^2} \\
&= \frac{18}{6\sqrt[3]{2}} * 10cms^{-1} &= \frac{30}{\sqrt[3]{2}} cms^{-1}\\
\end{aligned}
$$

### $(b)$

$$
\begin{aligned}
3m &= 30 * 10 cm \\
R(t) &= 30 \\
\sqrt[3]{18t} &= 30 \\
18t &= 30^3 \\
t &= \frac{30^3}{18} \\
&= 1500
\end{aligned}
$$
